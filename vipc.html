Before getting started with theses NCERT Solutions for Maths, Here are Some Must Follow Tips & Tricks for Practicing with Our Video Tutorials:

  • Distraction Free Study Environment

    Mathematics requires more concentration than any other subject. While following these NCERT video tutorials you need a distraction free study environment. Study in a peaceful and noise-free study room. It will help you in stimulating the flow of solving complex equations in geometry, algebra or trigonometry! Also, it is would be a good idea to study with your friends. You can practice in your study group. This way it becomes easy for you to bounce ideas off while solving critical problems.

  • Prepare Your Notes

    The notes preparation while watching the videos is simple yet most effective strategies. Make sure to keep a journal and prepare your notes on all the important points and formulas in that. It will help you to retain the information for a longer period of time. While, you can anytime come back to revise the videos with the expert!

  • Face the Doubts

    Maths is all about practice. If you find yourself stuck in one question then try to process the each step on a notebook. Write it down with a pencil or pen. Time and patience are two virtues to master the Mathematics. Soon writing on your own will turn out to be stepping stone in your numerical solving ability.
